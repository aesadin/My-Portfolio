About Me

My name is Allison Sadin, I am 29 years old and I am currently a student at Epicodus in Portland Oregon. I moved to Oregon 8 years ago with the intention of moving into community development and local government. However, before moving to Portland, I lived in California where I studied astronomy and astrophysics. When I moved to Portland, I began studying an under graduate degree in Political Science. Soon after graduating from Portland State University with my bachelors in hand, I realized politics was not the path for me.

Although my formal education is in political science, I always had an interest in STEM. I chose to move into programming so that I could exercise my love for science and creation, while harboring a deep sense of team-building and development. Ultimately I see myself leading, hopefully settling into a project management role in the longterm, but gaining experience with a dedicated group of like-minded programmers until then.
